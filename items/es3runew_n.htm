Runeword Changes
Melody, Malice, Steel, Strength, Wind, Zephir:
Enhanced Damage is doubled.
Venom:
Poison Damage and Poison Length are doubled.
Fury:
Enhanced Damage varies between 209% and 309%.
Silence:
Enhanced Damage varies between 200% and 300%.
Edge:
Reduce Vendor Price 15% is changed to 150% Extra Gold From Monsters.
Grief:
Damage +340-400 is changed to +340-400% Enhanced Damage.
Enigma, Chains of Honor:
Added +2 To All Skills. (+4 To All Skills in total)
Bramble:
+13 Life After Each Kill is changed to +2 To All Skills.
Lionheart, Wealth:
Added +1 To All Skills.
Duress, Gloom, Prudence, Stone, Sanctuary:
A junk mod is changed to +1 To All Skills.
Aura Item Bug Fix
Two Holy Aura items work separately and the slvls aren't added up to fix an
exploitable bug. This change may make some LoD runewords less effective.
