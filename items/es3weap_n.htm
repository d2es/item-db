Knuckles
Knuckle is a new weapon class added in R3D, which uses the bare-handed combat animation.
Though knuckles' range is short, the swing speed is faster than other weapons ( Calculator ).
Assassin can equip two knuckles. Claw Only skills (DC, FoF, CoT, BoI) work with Knuckles.
Knuckles are also treated as Blunt Weapons, and have the inherit +50% Damage bonus to Undead.
Battering Arms look 1H, but are too heavy to use with a shield, and treated as 2H weapons.
